ABOUT US

Providing full range of Transportation

Infix Logistics Zambia Is A Premier Provider Of Global Logistics Services Based In Lusaka, Zambia. With Years Of Industry Experience And A Commitment To Excellence, We Specialize In Delivering Integrated Logistics Solutions That Encompass Transportation With Our Own 20 Trucks & More Then 100 Trucks With Our Associate Partners, Customs Clearance At All The Entry Border Post, And Specialized Logistics Services. Our Strategic Location In Lusaka Allows Us To Efficiently Connect Businesses Across Zambia To Global Markets, Ensuring Seamless Movement Of Goods And Materials.

To Provide Superior Global Logistics Services That Empower Businesses, Foster Economic Growth, And Enhance International Trade, While Maintaining The Highest Standards Of Integrity And Reliability.

TECHNOLOGY INTEGRATION

WE LEVERAGE CUTTING-EDGE TECHNOLOGY AND ADVANCED DATA ANALYTICS TO OPTIMIZE LOGISTICS PROCESSES

1. Advanced Tracking Systems

Advanced Tracking Systems

We utilize gps tracking systems and rfid (radio frequency identification) tags to monitor the precise location of shipments in real-time. This technology allows us to provide accurate updates to our customers regarding the status and eta of their shipments.

2. Digital Platforms and Integration

Cloud-based Solutions

Our operations are supported by cloud-based platforms that enable seamless communication and data sharing across all departments and with our clients. This ensures transparency and real-time access to critical logistics information.

3. Data Analytics for Optimization

Predictive Analytics

We employ predictive analytics models to forecast demand, optimize inventory levels, and plan efficient routes. This proactive approach minimizes delays and reduces transportation costs.

Machine Learning Algorithms

Machine learning algorithms analyze historical data to identify patterns and optimize logistics operations. This includes route planning, warehouse management, and inventory replenishment, improving overall efficiency.

4. Real-Time Visibility and Customer Portal

Customer Portal

Through a secure online portal, our customers have real-time visibility into their shipments, allowing them to track progress, receive alerts, and access detailed analytics reports.

Mobile Applications

Mobile apps provide on-the-go access to shipment tracking and status updates, enhancing convenience and transparency for our customers.
